About Our Website

Welcome to our Garbage Management System! Our main goal is to improve cleanliness in urban areas by providing an easy and efficient way for residents to raise complaints about garbage collection.

Through our platform, users can report uncollected garbage online, and our team ensures timely collection and disposal. We are committed to making our cities cleaner and healthier by addressing waste management issues efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage technology to create a sustainable and hygienic environment by ensuring proper garbage collection and disposal in urban areas.

How It Works

  • Users submit complaints regarding uncollected garbage.
  • Our system processes and forwards complaints to the relevant authorities.
  • A dedicated team ensures timely collection and disposal.
  • Users can track the progress of their complaints online.
